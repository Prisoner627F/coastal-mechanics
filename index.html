About Coastal Mechanics

THE EXPERTISE
OF A HIGH-END
SHOP.

With three years of dedicated experience specialising in the precision of Japanese engineering and the complexity of European imports, I bring the expertise of a high-end shop directly to your driveway. Whether you are driving a reliable daily commuter or a high-performance German machine, I provide meticulous maintenance and repair tailored to your vehicle's specific needs.

I founded this business on a simple principle: Professional service shouldn't require a waiting room. I focus on clear communication, transparent pricing, and the convenience of getting you back on the road without the dealership hassle.
